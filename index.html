Microschool

Bison Prairie Farms Microschool is a nature-inspired learning community for homeschooling families who want to nurture curiosity, creativity, and a deep connection with the natural world. Designed for children K-5, our program blends outdoor exploration with hands-on, interdisciplinary lessons rooted in science, art, and storytelling.

Enrollment Options

  • Part Time: An enrichment group for peer interaction, nature play, themed unit studies, and play opportunities to support your homeschool program.
  • Full Time: Includes all part-time benefits, plus formal instruction in core content areas (Math and ELA).

Nature Enrichment Program

Join us on an exciting adventure into the heart of nature! Bison Prairie Farms Nature Enrichment Program is a fun and interactive program designed for curious kids who love the outdoors. Each week, young adventurers will embark on a journey to uncover the secrets of the outdoors. They'll learn about animals, plants, and ecosystems through hands-on activities like bug hunts, leaf crafts, birdwatching, and nature storytelling.
