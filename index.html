Childcare

At Bison Prairie Farms, we believe that the great outdoors is the best classroom for young minds. Nestled within trees and surrounded by open spaces and wildlife, our program offers a nurturing environment where children connect with nature and develop a deep sense of curiosity, wonder, and respect for the world around them.

Our nature-based early learning curriculum encourages exploration, hands-on learning, and creativity through outdoor activities like gardening, hiking, building, and animal care. Children engage with natural elements like mud, water, plants, and wildlife, fostering physical, emotional, and cognitive development in ways that are meaningful and fun.

Through unstructured play and guided discovery, we promote skills such as problem-solving, teamwork, and environmental stewardship. Whether it’s collecting leaves, building a fort, or watching the growth of a planted seed, every day offers new opportunities for growth and exploration.

We create a warm, supportive community where children learn to respect each other and the environment.

Microschool

Bison Prairie Farms Microschool is a nature-inspired learning community for homeschooling families who want to nurture curiosity, creativity, and a deep connection with the natural world. Designed for children K-5, our program blends outdoor exploration with hands-on, interdisciplinary lessons rooted in science, art, and storytelling.

Enrollment Options

  • Part-Time (1-2 Days a Week): An enrichment group for peer interaction, nature play, themed unit studies, and play opportunities to support your homeschool program.
  • Full-Time (4-5 Days a Week): Along with peer interaction, nature play, and unit studies, full-time students will also receive formal instruction in core content areas (Math and ELA).

Homeschool Consultation

Are you considering homeschooling but not sure where to start? Are you tired of searching endlessly only to end up overwhelmed with options and confused about what you need to keep track of?

Our Homeschool Consultation Service is here to guide you every step of the way. Especially for those who are new to homeschooling, we offer personalized support designed to help you build a well-rounded, engaging, and effective learning environment.

This service is available to Kansas families. For $99, you’ll get up to 90 minutes of one-on-one conversation to help navigate how to withdraw from school, what paperwork needs filed, local resources available, help in choosing curricula that fit your needs, and support in organizing and structuring your schedule.

Broken down into two sessions, this support is designed to empower you to feel confident in your choice to homeschool.

Nature Enrichment Program

Join us on an exciting adventure into the heart of nature! Bison Prairie Farms Nature Enrichment Program is a fun and interactive program designed for curious kids who love the outdoors. Each week, young adventurers will embark on a journey to uncover the secrets of the outdoors. They'll learn about animals, plants, and ecosystems through hands-on activities like bug hunts, leaf crafts, birdwatching, and nature storytelling.

Led by experienced teachers, this program encourages curiosity, creativity, and environmental stewardship, teaching kids how to appreciate and protect the world around them. Perfect for ages 5–12, the Nature Enrichment Program is a great way to spark a lifelong love of nature!

About Me & Contact

Kylie Kilmer, MS.Ed
Owner / Lead Instructor
TSVI/COMS

I have a Bachelor’s Degree in recreation therapy and a Master’s Degree in special education. As a former special education teacher, I have 7 years of experience working with children of all ages and abilities. Education was great, but I needed to make a change to staying at home after having our children. This program allows me to get back to what I loved about teaching — the kids!

I am married to Austin, who works in the technology industry and is a part-time law enforcement officer. Together, we have 3 children as part of a blended family.

When our child struggled with the transition to a Kindergarten classroom, we took the leap into homeschooling. After hearing story after story of families with the same experience, I decided to create a program that was a more natural shift into learning and offered all the movement and play our kids still needed. Our child was finished with academic instruction every day by lunch and was soaring! I knew we were onto something...

I believe in setting clear boundaries and expectations without raising my voice, and I allow children to learn to make safe choices on their own. Natural consequences are my preferred way to handle discipline. We will take responsible risks, make messes, practice using calm words to express wants and needs, work on self-reliance skills, make choices for ourselves, and explore our social/emotional skills through real-life conflict management.

I look forward to working with your family, and I can’t wait to see this generation of kids grow up to change the world!
